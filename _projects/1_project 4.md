Financial auditing is an exhaustive examination task performed by an auditing company for (profif or non-profit) financial entities to guarantee the correctness of financial statements.  Funded by NWO and KPMG, this project focuses on developing novel explainable neuro-symbolic AI algorithms to support human auditing experts. Dear project participiants are Prof. Dr. Alessandra Palmigiano (VU) (project PI), Marcel Boersma (KPMG & UvA), Khrishna Manoorkar (VU) and Mattia Pannettiere (VU). 

Early preprints: 

- [**A Meta-Algorithm for Interrogative Agendas**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.01837), [KR4HI 2022](https://sites.google.com/view/kr4hi/programme), Erman Acar, Andrea De Domenico, Krishna Manoorkar and Mattia Panettiere, 2022 (KR4HI 2022 (to appear))
- [**Flexible Categorization for Auditing using Formal Concept Analysis and Dempster-Shafer theory**](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2210.17330.pdf), Marcel Boersma, Krishna Manoorkar, Alessandra Palmigiano, Mattia Panettiere, Apostolos Tzimoulis, and Nachoem Wijnberg (ArXiv 2022)
